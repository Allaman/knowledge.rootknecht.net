## Configuring mouse and trackpoint

List devices with `xinput --list'

List props of a device with `xinput --list-props <DEVICE_ID>`

Set acceleration with `xinput --set-prop <DEVICE ID> 'libinput Accel Speed' <ACC FACTOR>`
